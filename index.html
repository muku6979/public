Taj Rishikesh Resort & Spa, Uttarakhand

On 5 hillside hectares along the banks of the Ganges, this tranquil, upscale resort lies just off the NH7. It's 13 km from both Vashishtha Guha Temple and Arundhati Cave.

Airy, chic rooms, most with river views, offer flat-screen TVs, minibars and safes, plus rainfall showers and complimentary Wi-Fi. Upgraded rooms have living areas and/or terraces.
